One of North Star Law's main focus is looking after non-domiciliaries in the UK. Whether the client is a high net-worth individual, entrepreneur or just a young person venturing abroad in the hope of realising their dreams we can help. We help clients move to the UK, while they are in the UK and help them with their departure from the UK. North Star Law’s interest in each client means we are on call to "hold the client’s hand".

North Star Law regularly acts on prestigious commercial and high value residential property transactions. The team prides itself on the quality not the quantity of their work.

We aim to make the process as easy and uncomplicated for the client as possible. We assist you at every stage of the process from negotiation to handover of the keys – and beyond if requested.

Our immigration team help individuals and businesses from all round the world to gain the correct permissions to come to or remain in the UK. We provide strategic immigration advice at every step of the journey in order to help our clients achieve their long-term goals in the UK.
